BANES & BOONS

BANES

BANES

Banes: Negative conditions that your character inflict upon its foes, such as by stunning them, demoralizing them, or setting them on fire.
VS: What attribute is used when invoking the bane and what attribute the foe uses to defend.
Dice: The attribute dice.
Description: Effects of the Bane, for example your foe cannot see anything for 1 turn.
Special: Special conditions that influences the bane. For example how to remove the bane.

Banes Vs Dice Description Special

BOONS

BOONS

Boons: Helpful effects that assist your character's allies by allowing them to fly, shrug off damage, or move with extraordinary speed.
VS: What attribute is used when invoking the boon and what attribute the foe uses to defend.
Dice: The attribute dice.
Description: Effects of the Boon, for example your foe cannot see anything for 1 turn.
Special: Special conditions that influences the boon. For example if other character's can be affected by it.
