Rules

Attributes

Attributes detimines your characters capabillitys regarding certian fileds.

The attributes that the character picks affect other areas aswell such as the Feats and Flaws category.

Feats

Feats defiene things that your character excel at. This can include things such as reflexes on the battlefiled. Each Feat costs a certain amount om featpoints. At level 1 the player has a total of 6 featpoints, and the Feats cost range from 1-3. Certain feats require the character to posses a ceratin attribute such as might or agility. Typically a player has 2-3 feats at the beginning of a game.

Perks and Flaws

Perks describe the characters positive features and characteristics. Perks says how your charachter responds i ceartin situations, examples of this is how the players charachter reponds to a treat or to a person who is in need of help. Flaws however determine a characters negative traits. This ranges from addiction to bloodlust and they to play a part in the respons a character gives based on the situation and enviroment. The player MUST have an equal amount of perks and flaws.

Banes and Boons

Banes says what the players charachter inflict upon a foe(negative effect on enemy). All banes cannot be picked by all types of characters as a requierd amount such as might must be possesd. The player then chooses the attribute and rolls it against a foes attribute ex. roll your might against their tougness. Bones determines a characthers ability to help allies on the battlefiled.
